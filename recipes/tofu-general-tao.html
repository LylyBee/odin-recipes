Tofu General Tao

Tofu, a great ally for vegan recipes

Tofu genreal Tao is a plant-based dish composed of fried-tofu, sweet sauce and bunching onions. It is served with basmati rice for best results.

Ingredients

  • Tofu (1 bloc)
  • Bunching onions (3)
  • Garlic clove(1)
  • Canola oil
  • Tamari sauce (4 Tbs)
  • Soy sauce (2 Tbs)
  • Vineger(3 Tbs)
  • Sesame oil (1 1/2 Tsp)
  • Ketchup (4 Tbs)
  • Cornstarsh (7 Tbs)
  • Water (1 cup)
  • Cayenne pepper (facultative)
  • Sesame seeds (for presentation)

Preparation

Tofu

  1. Cut the tofu in cubes.
  2. Put 4 Tbs of cornstarsh in a bowl, add the tofu and stir.
  3. Preheat some canola oil in a pan and fry the tofu.

Sauce

  1. In a pan, roast the bunching onions and the garlic clove for 3-5 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, in a measuring cup (or a bowl), pour the water, the tamari sauce, the soy sauce, the vinegar, the sesam oil, the ketchup and the remaining cornstarch (3 Tbs) and stir.
  3. Pour the content of the measuring cup in the pan and stir until the sauce thickens.
